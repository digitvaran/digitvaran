Digit-Varan aims to overcome problems faced by visually challenged students in undergraduate learning institutions. It is a comprehensive information acquisition, storage and delivery system, designed specifically to fulfil requirements of visually challenged students of colleges of University of Delhi.The website has been designed using W3C - AA norms to make it accessible for visually challenged and has been extensively tested by visually challenged students. It is an electronic notice board designed specifically for visually challenged students. It has built interaction protocols with St. Stephen's College's societies. DIGIT VARAN Team
